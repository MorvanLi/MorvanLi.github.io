About me

I am an incoming Ph.D. candidate in Computer Technology at Xi'an Jiaotong University, advised by Prof. Jun Liu. Before that, I obtained the MSc. degree and the BSc. degree from the Computer Science, Xihua university, China. supervised by Prof. Hong Peng. My research is mainly related to image fusion computer vision and membrane computing. I am also broadly interested in natural language processing and logical reasoning in general.
